Privacy Policy Last updated: April 8, 2026 StrideMind ("we," "our," or "the app") is a cognitive training app built for active adults. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights around it. Who We Are StrideMind is an independently developed app. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at appstridemind@gmail.com. What We Collect Account information: your name and email address when you create an account. Usage data: the training sessions you complete, their duration, and your activity history within the app. Subscription status: whether you have an active premium subscription, managed through RevenueCat. We do not collect health data, location data, or any device sensor data. How We Use Your Data To display your training history and progress inside the app. To calculate your weekly training progress toward your goal. To manage your account and subscription status. To send optional reminders through local notifications on your device (only if you enable them). These notifications are generated locally and do not transmit any data to our servers. We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. Data Security All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Your account data is stored securely through Supabase, which maintains its own security certifications and practices. Third-Party Services StrideMind uses the following third-party services: Supabase — database and authentication. Your account data and session history are stored here. Supabase Privacy Policy RevenueCat — subscription management. Purchase and entitlement data is processed here. RevenueCat Privacy Policy Expo — app development platform used to build and deliver the app. Expo Privacy Policy Apple — payment processing for in-app subscriptions is handled entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details. International Data Transfers StrideMind uses Supabase, which stores data on infrastructure hosted in the United States (Amazon Web Services). If you are located outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. Where required by applicable law, we rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards to legitimize such transfers. By using the app, you acknowledge that your data may be processed in the United States. Data Retention Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days. Your Rights (All Users) Regardless of where you are located, you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at appstridemind@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. Right to delete — you can request that we delete your personal data. Right to opt out of sale — we do not sell your personal data to third parties. Right to non-discrimination — we will not treat you differently for exercising any of these rights. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at appstridemind@gmail.com. Right to restriction — you can ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances. Right to data portability — you can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
