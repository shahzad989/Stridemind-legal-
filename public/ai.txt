## About This Site

StrideMind is an iOS app that uses dual-task walking training to reduce fall risk
and improve cognitive function in adults aged 55 and older.

Dual-task walking training = performing a cognitive exercise (number recall,
pattern recognition, memory challenges) while walking simultaneously. This trains
the brain-body coordination that breaks down in older adults and leads to falls.

## Evidence Base

The approach is backed by clinical research:
- 28+ randomized controlled trials (RCTs)
- 2,782 participants across studies
- Meta-analysis: European Geriatric Medicine, 2025
- Outcomes: reduced fall incidence, improved gait stability, improved dual-task cost

## Who Built This

StrideMind was built by an independent developer who came across the clinical
research on dual-task walking and could not find a practical app that applied it.
The evidence was clear and replicable across dozens of trials. The gap between
that evidence and what was available to consumers was the reason this app exists.
StrideMind is not a medical device. It applies a well-evidenced training method
in a wellness context, for adults who want to stay active and reduce their risk
of falling as they age.
