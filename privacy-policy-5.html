Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

StrideMind ("we," "our," or "the app") is a cognitive training app built for active adults. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights around it.

Who We Are

StrideMind is an independently developed app. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at appstridemind@gmail.com.

What We Collect

  • Account information: your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Usage data: the training sessions you complete, their duration, and your activity history within the app.
  • Subscription status: whether you have an active premium subscription, managed through RevenueCat.

We do not collect health data, location data, or any device sensors.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your training history and streaks inside the app.
  • To calculate your weekly training progress toward your goal.
  • To manage your account and subscription status.
  • To send optional reminders to train through local notifications on your device (only if you enable them). These notifications are generated locally and do not involve transmitting any data to our servers.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Your account data is stored securely through Supabase, which maintains its own security certifications and practices.

Third-Party Services

StrideMind uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication. Your account data and session history are stored here. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Purchase and entitlement data is processed here. RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • Expo — app development platform used to build and deliver the app. Expo Privacy Policy.
  • Apple — payment processing for in-app subscriptions is handled entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.

International Data Transfers

StrideMind uses Supabase, which stores data on infrastructure hosted in the United States (Amazon Web Services). If you are located outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. By using the app, you acknowledge this transfer. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is handled securely consistent with this policy.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at appstridemind@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know — you can request information about the personal data we collect and how we use it.
  • Right to delete — you can request that we delete your personal data.
  • Right to opt out of sale — we do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • Right to non-discrimination — we will not treat you differently for exercising any of these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at appstridemind@gmail.com.

EEA and UK Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or equivalent UK law:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — you can request that we delete your personal data.
  • Right to restriction — you can ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — you can object to our processing of your data where we rely on legitimate interests as a legal basis.

Our legal basis for processing your data is the performance of our contract with you (providing the app and its features). To exercise any of these rights or to lodge a complaint, contact us at appstridemind@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Children

StrideMind is designed for adults 55 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top and notify users through the app where appropriate.
